Section 1a-1c 对话

A:I want to join the music club
B:Oh,can you sing?
A: Yes,I can.

A:Can you swim?
B:No,I can't.

A:I want to join the art club
B:Can you draw?
A:Yes,I can.

——要点点评:

1.I want to join the art club
①want to do 想去（做) do可用行为动词代替，do用代替的动词的原型，如I want to dance:我想跳舞
②冠词+学科内容+club 如a sports club;the music club

2.Can you draw?
①Can 助动词后接动词用原形
②冠词+学科内容+club 如a sports club;the music club

回答用: Yes/No,主语 can('t(否定句时用))
